## 1. Color Profile: "Ascend"

The chosen color palette balances **energy (Fitness)**, **calm (Wellness)**, and **trust/precision (AI & Data)** to create a modern, easy-to-use interface. All colors are selected with **accessibility** and optimal performance in both Light and Dark Modes in mind.

| Category | Color Name | Hex Code | Rationale & UX Purpose |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Primary/Accent** | **Ascend Blue** | `#007AFF` | The standard, trustworthy iOS blue. Used for **CTAs, active states, main navigation, and the AI Companion's chat bubbles**. Signifies trust and focus. |
| **Secondary/Energy** | **Vitality Teal** | `#00C896` | A fresh, invigorating color. Used exclusively for **Fitness, Activity, Workout Tracking, and Community content** related to exercise. Conveys energy and growth. |
| **Tertiary/Calm** | **Serenity Lavender** | `#B58BEF` | A soft, gentle color. Used for **Wellness, Mood Tracking, Meditation reminders, and Rest Day suggestions**. Signifies mindfulness and introspection. |
| **Neutral/Foundation** | **Clean Slate** | `#FFFFFF` / `#1C1C1E` (Dark) | Ensures maximum **readability and clarity**. White for Light Mode backgrounds; Dark Gray for Dark Mode backgrounds (following iOS system UI). |
| **Data/Success** | **Growth Green** | `#34C759` | Standard green for **positive feedback, goal completion, and success messages**. |
| **Alert/Warning** | **Attention Orange** | `#FF9500` | Used sparingly for **urgent alerts, warnings**, or goals/metrics needing immediate attention. |

## 2. Icon Strategy and Selection

The icon must be simple, recognizable, and immediately communicate the app's holistic focus on the three pillars: Fitness, Wellness, and Nutrition/AI.

### ðŸ¥‡ **Selected Icon Option: The Holistic Cycle**

* **Concept:** A single, continuous loop/cycle representing the integration and completion of the three health pillars.
* **Design:** A thick, continuous circular line composed of three distinct colored arcs.
    * **Arc 1 (Fitness):** Vitality Teal
    * **Arc 2 (Wellness):** Serenity Lavender
    * **Arc 3 (Nutrition/AI):** Ascend Blue
* **Rationale:** This option is the strongest visual metaphor for the app's value proposition: **balance and interconnectedness**. It is modern, distinct, and highly scalable for both the iOS Home Screen and the Apple Watch complication.

## 3. Future Considerations & UX Notes

* **Dark Mode Priority:** Ensure the **Ascend Blue** has sufficient contrast against the Dark Mode background (`#1C1C1E`) for optimal accessibility (AA standard).
* **AI Companion Branding:** Use **Ascend Blue** consistently for all AI-generated text or prompts to establish the AI's identity as trustworthy and data-driven.
* **Community Segmentation:** Use the primary feature colors (**Vitality Teal** for workout shares and **Serenity Lavender** for mindfulness posts) as subtle visual tags or borders in the Community feed to help users quickly filter and categorize content.
