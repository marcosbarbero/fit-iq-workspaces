## üÜò Troubleshooting

### "I don't see my data after 5 minutes"

1. **Check your internet connection:**
   - Make sure you're connected to Wi-Fi or cellular
   - Try opening Safari to verify internet works

2. **Force quit and reopen the app:**
   - Swipe up from bottom (or double-click home button)
   - Swipe up on FitIQ to close it
   - Tap FitIQ to reopen

3. **Check HealthKit permissions:**
   - Open **Settings** app
   - Scroll to **Health**
   - Tap **Data Access & Devices**
   - Tap **FitIQ**
   - Make sure all toggles are **ON**

### "I forgot my password"

1. On the sign-in screen, tap **"Forgot Password?"**
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for reset instructions
4. Follow the link to create a new password
5. Return to app and sign in with new password

### "HealthKit permission request didn't appear"

1. Open **Settings** app
2. Scroll to **FitIQ**
3. Tap **HealthKit**
4. Enable all health data categories you want to share
5. Return to FitIQ app
6. Pull down to refresh the Summary view

### "The app is stuck on loading screen"

1. Force quit the app:
   - Swipe up from bottom (or double-click home button)
   - Swipe up on FitIQ to close it
2. Reopen the app
3. If still stuck, delete and reinstall again (data is safe!)

## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Will I lose any data?
**A:** No! All your data is safely stored:
- Progress tracking data is in our cloud
- HealthKit data remains in Apple Health
- Everything re-syncs automatically after reinstall

### Q: Why is this necessary?
**A:** We fixed a critical bug in how the app stores data locally. The only way to apply this fix to existing installations is to start fresh with the corrected database structure.

### Q: Will I need to do this again in the future?
**A:** No! This is a **one-time** requirement. All future updates will install seamlessly without needing to delete/reinstall.

### Q: How long will sync take?
**A:** Typically 1-3 minutes. The app syncs:
1. Your account info (immediate)
2. Your progress data from our servers (30 seconds)
3. Your HealthKit data from Apple Health (1-2 minutes)

### Q: What if I have a lot of historical data?
**A:** No problem! The app will sync all your historical data automatically. It may take slightly longer (3-5 minutes) if you have months of data, but everything will come back.

### Q: Can I reinstall on multiple devices?
**A:** Yes! Sign in with the same account on all your devices. Your data will sync across all of them.

### Q: What happens to my meal photos?
**A:** Meal photos are stored in the cloud and will re-sync automatically after you sign in.
